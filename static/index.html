#socialpoetry

social poetry is a simple excercise in creativity that yields unusual results. the rules are following:

  • pick a hashtag.
  • wait until we load a list of tweets containing this hashtag for you.
  • try to compose a poem by chosing tweets and reorganizing them.
  • only minor editorial changes are allowed: adding whitespace & interpunction, removing unecessary hashtags / mentions and switching lower/upper case.
  • after you are done, you can save the result into our database and share the link to it with rest of the world.
